Greetings from Perseverance Technology

October 24, 2025 · 2 min read

Welcome to the official blog of Perseverance Technology! We’re excited to share our work in innovation. Now we're introducing our flagship product: Unicorn Solution. At Perseverance Technology, our goal is simple: build technology that matters. Unicorn Solution is the result of this mission—a market-ready safety system designed for Heavy Goods Vehicles (HGVs).

It's an advanced camera monitoring application that addresses critical MOIS (Moving Off Information System) and BSIS (Blind Spot Information System) requirements, fully compliant with UN R151 and UN R159. What sets it apart? It achieves all this using a single fisheye camera paired with powerful AI-based detection.

Inside Unicorn Solution​

Unicorn Solution is a complete visualization platform designed for safety and operational efficiency. Its key features include:

  • Live View Mode: A real-time monitoring system with various display layouts, including MOIS, BSIS, Panorama, and Original.
  • Playback Mode: Displays all recorded results from Live View, allowing users to review and analyze past footage.
  • Configuration Mode: Allows users to adjust camera parameters, display preferences, and system behavior.
  • Setting Mode: Enables customization of operational settings, including Camera Parameters, Resolution, and Language.
  • Help Mode: Provides guidance on the functions of all buttons in the interface to help users understand the system.

Join Us on the Journey​

Stay tuned for fresh updates, deep dives, and stories from the people behind the work. This blog will be your hub for updates, technical insights, and support for Unicorn Solution.

Thank you for being part of this community—together, we’ll build great things!
