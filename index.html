Real-Time Monitoring

Unicorn Solution provides real-time visualization from fisheye cameras, allowing users to monitor vehicle surroundings through multiple display modes such as MOIS, BSIS, Original, and Panorama.

Smart Configuration

Easily adjust camera parameters, view alignment, and calibration settings through an intuitive Configuration Mode. Each camera can be fine-tuned for optimal image accuracy and seamless panoramic integration.

AI-Based Safety System

Equipped with AI-powered detection, Unicorn Solution helps identify pedestrians, cyclists, and obstacles in blind spots to support safer vehicle operation and compliance with safety regulations.
